My Designs

     

The design world nowadays in a very crowded market but it’s only crowded as so many people try it but cannot master it; design is a skill that involves mixing type, colour and images to create visual image that can portray some sort of message. I always try to do colourful but unique designs and when I’m making a logo or something for a website I always try and think of the brand as a whole and how people will perceive the brand by the logo.
